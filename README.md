## Analytics for the Pilot Mall
Download .pbix [here](https://github.com/chvladimirova/portfolio/blob/main/dashboards/Mall_dashboard.pbix)

<img src="https://github.com/chulpanvl00/chulpanvl00/assets/69518110/1c7384b5-8178-46eb-83ae-48b9101a1be3" width="600" height="340" >

#### Objective
To give a Managing Director a high-level overview of the mall's performance providing insights into space allocation, tenants' leasing costs, and foot traffic patterns.
#### Key Insights
- **Understand Space Allocation:** Analyze key metrics like Gross Leasable Area (GLA) and occupancy rates by floor to identify leasing opportunities and optimize rental income.
- **Evaluate Tenant Profitability:** Deep dive into individual tenant lease details, including contract duration, fixed rent, and operating expenses, to assess profitability and optimize lease negotiations.
- **Identify Top Performers:** Gain insights into daily foot traffic for each tenant, allowing you to identify top performers and understand what drives customer traffic within the mall.
#### Technical Features
- **Interactive map** of the mall (using Synoptic panel).

## EVS Analytics
Download .pbix [here](https://github.com/chvladimirova/portfolio/blob/main/dashboards/EVS_dashboard.pbix).

<img src="https://github.com/chulpanvl00/PowerBI-dashboards/assets/69518110/65f24140-77e6-4253-bea7-b67ae31fe056" width="600" height="340" >

#### Objective
This comprehensive dashboard delves into the performance of company-owned electric vehicle charging stations (EVS) from January 1st, 2021, to December 31st, 2022. It provides a granular analysis of charging session metrics, including quantity, duration, and revenue generated per location, empowering you to optimize EVS operations and maximize profitability.
#### Key Insights
- **Revenue Analysis:** Analyze the total income generated by charging stations to assess their financial contribution.
- **EV Stations Efficiency:** Evaluate the number of charging sessions to understand usage patterns; identify the average cost per charging minute and average session duration to optimize pricing strategies and improve customer experience.

## KPI Analytics
<img src="https://github.com/chvladimirova/chvladimirova/assets/69518110/7cdd5ed4-a59d-47b8-8969-09bf73443183" width="360" height="230" > <img src="https://github.com/chvladimirova/chvladimirova/assets/69518110/39277ddf-c793-4e6a-b28a-8a1d6025cf8a" width="360" height="230" > 

#### Objective
This interactive dashboard empowers department managers and HR personnel with a comprehensive overview of key performance indicators (KPIs) tailored to their specific areas of responsibility.  It provides department managers with a data-driven foundation for evaluating employee performance and determining annual bonuses.
#### Key Insights
- **Track Departmental Progress:** Monitor key department-specific KPIs to assess progress towards departmental goals. Identify areas where teams might require additional support or process optimization.
- **Monitor Individual and Team Performance:** Track both individual employee performance metrics and overall departmental KPIs through interactive charts and graphs. This comprehensive view allows managers to understand how individual contributions translate to team and departmental success.
- **Data-Driven Bonus Decisions:** Department managers leverage insights from departmental and employee-level KPIs to make informed decisions regarding the allocation of annual bonuses.
#### Technical Features
- Row-level security (**RLS**) to ensure a secure access to sensitive employee data.
- Connection to **MongoDB** as a data source.

## Cashflow Analytics
<img src="https://github.com/chulpanvl00/chulpanvl00/assets/69518110/21a2d69e-c325-4b35-8a62-8f270b60e2f1" width="360" height="230" > <img src="https://github.com/chulpanvl00/chulpanvl00/assets/69518110/c060e665-5e6f-4183-af77-002f9667945d" width="360" height="230" >

#### Objective
This cash flow analysis dashboard equips the Financial department with the insights necessary to make informed decisions regarding financial planning, budgeting, and cash flow management strategies.
#### Key Insights
- **Net Cash Flow Analysis:** Gain a quick understanding of the company's overall cash flow health by analyzing the ending cash balance and net cash flow for the period.
- **Plan vs. Actual Comparison:** Evaluate the effectiveness of financial planning by comparing planned cash flow for each activity type (operating, investing, financing) against actual figures. Identify variances and potential areas for improvement.
- **Financial Stability:** Monitor external revenue receipts, a key indicator of financial health, to ensure the company's ability to meet obligations and fuel growth. 

## Accounts Receivable Analytics
<img src="https://github.com/chulpanvl00/chulpanvl00/assets/69518110/d4d0b921-effd-4ad4-82f5-0c1ece22df6c" width="360" height="230" > <img src="https://github.com/chulpanvl00/chulpanvl00/assets/69518110/7ba09967-503e-4599-abc8-4f0d82ec6055" width="360" height="230" >
<img src="https://github.com/chulpanvl00/chulpanvl00/assets/69518110/31d2dd73-7b1b-4d95-acff-4aa39414d21c" width="360" height="230" > <img src="https://github.com/chulpanvl00/chulpanvl00/assets/69518110/aad8359e-70ba-4e1f-8c16-d31b10f548de" width="360" height="230" >
#### Objective
This interactive accounts receivable (AR) analysis dashboard empowers the Financial department to gain a comprehensive understanding of the company's AR portfolio.
#### Key Insights
- **AR Health Monitoring:** Gain a quick overview of the AR health by analyzing the total AR balance, current vs. past due balances, and the average collection period.
- **Detailed AR Breakdown:** Evaluate the AR details for each legal entity, including the total AR balance, current and past due balances, and potentially detailed aging schedules.
- **Concentration Risk Assessment:** Leverage drill-down capabilities to assess the risk associated with AR concentration across legal entities and counterparty types (internal vs. external debtors). Analyze the distribution of AR balances to identify potential risks associated with a high dependence on a single customer or debtor type.
#### Technical Features
- Power BI's **drill-down** capabilities to navigate from high-level overviews to detailed insights for specific legal entities and counterparty types.
